1. **Areas & Tasks**
   - Operatories: pre/post-patient wipe-down, barriers, instrument transport.
   - Sterilization: ultrasonic, packaging, autoclave cycles, spore tests.
   - Common areas: reception, bathrooms, staff room.
   - Equipment: suction lines, compressors, chairs, X‑ray heads.
   - High-touch surfaces: door handles, keyboards, pens, POS terminals.

2. **Standards & Frequencies**
   - Chair/operatory surfaces: between every patient.
   - Instruments: per load, with logged cycle parameters.
   - Suction line cleaning: daily (plus weekly shock treatment).
   - Spore testing: weekly (or per local law).
   - Deep cleaning: weekly/monthly checklists.

3. **Digital Tracking System**
   - Roles: Admin, Dentist, Hygienist, Assistant, Front Desk, Cleaner.
   - Each task:  
     - Area  
     - Procedure (step list)  
     - Required product/contact time  
     - Frequency (per patient/daily/weekly/monthly/as needed)  
     - Responsible role  
     - Time window (e.g., “within 10 min after patient checkout”)  
   - Interface:  
     - Dashboard with overdue/coming-due tasks.  
     - Color codes: green (done), yellow (due soon), red (overdue).  
     - Quick-tap completion with initials and timestamp.

4. **Checklists & Workflows**
   - **Per-patient operatory turnover**  
     - Remove disposables/barriers.  
     - Wipe all clinical contact surfaces with EPA-registered disinfectant (respect contact time).  
     - Replace barriers.  
     - Log completion tied to patient appointment ID.
   - **Instrument reprocessing**  
     - Log: patient batch, pre-cleaning, packaging, autoclave cycle #, parameters, operator initials.  
     - Attach spore test results to specific autoclave ID.
   - **Open/Close routines**  
     - Morning: verify sterilization indicators, stock PPE, run water lines.  
     - Evening: suction line cleaner, surface disinfection, trash removal, sharps check.

5. **Compliance & “Audits”**
   - Auto-generate daily/weekly reports: missed tasks, late tasks, who “completed” them.  
   - Exportable logs for inspections.  
   - Random spot-check prompts: require photo upload (e.g., sterilization indicator strip, cleaned room).

6. **Inventory & Products**
   - Track disinfectants, sterilization pouches, indicators, spore tests, PPE.  
   - Link tasks to specific products and lot numbers.  
   - Alerts when stock or expiration dates approach limits.

7. **Training & SOPs**
   - Embed SOPs and short videos in each task.  
   - Require staff to pass short quizzes before being allowed to “complete” certain tasks (e.g., sterilization).

8. **Implementation Steps**
   - Map your current workflow and legal requirements.  
   - Build task library and assign roles/frequencies.  
   - Pilot with one team for 2–4 weeks, then refine.  
   - Roll out to full office; review metrics monthly.

   - **Operatory (per patient)**  
     - Remove disposables/barriers.  
     - Clean/disinfect all clinical contact surfaces (chair, light handles, trays, X‑ray control, keyboards with covers).  
     - Replace barriers.  
     - Log tied to appointment ID.
   - **Instrument Reprocessing (per load)**  
     - Pre-clean (chairside or holding solution).  
     - Ultrasonic/washer-disinfector cycle.  
     - Dry, inspect, package with indicators.  
     - Autoclave: cycle #, parameters (time/temp/pressure), load ID, operator.  
     - Log chemical indicator result; link to spore test when applicable.
   - **Sterilization Monitoring**  
     - Biological spore test: weekly or per manufacturer/law.  
     - Log autoclave ID, cycle, date, result, corrective actions if failed.
   - **Daily Tasks**  
     - Suction line cleaner; waterline flush; surface disinfection of reception, bathrooms, staff areas; trash removal; sharps container level check.  
   - **Weekly/Monthly**  
     - Deep clean operatories and common areas.  
     - Shock treatment for waterlines (if used).  
     - Equipment cleaning (compressor, vacuum filters per manufacturer).  
     - Check expiry dates on disinfectants, indicators, spore tests, PPE.

3. **Digital System Features**
   - **Dashboard**  
     - Filter by date, area, role, status (due soon/overdue/completed).  
     - Color coding: green (on time), yellow (due within set window), red (overdue).  
   - **Scheduling Engine**  
     - Recurrence rules:  
       - Per-patient (triggered by appointment completion).  
       - Time-based (open/close, hourly, daily, weekly, monthly).  
       - Event-based (new autoclave installed, spore test failure).  
   - **Completion UX**  
     - One-tap “complete” with initials and auto-timestamp.  
     - Optional photo upload (e.g., sterilization indicator strip, cleaned room, autoclave printout).  
     - Quick notes for exceptions (e.g., “autoclave cycle aborted, re-ran cycle #45B”).
   - **Role-Based Access**  
     - Admin: configure tasks, view/edit all logs, export reports.  
     - Dentist/Owner: view compliance, sign off on incidents.  
     - Hygienist/Assistant: complete clinical tasks, view SOPs.  
     - Sterilization Tech: full access to reprocessing/sterilization modules.  
     - Front Desk: reception/common-area tasks.  
     - Cleaning Staff: after-hours cleaning checklists only.

4. **SOPs & Training Integration**
   - Each task links to:  
     - Step-by-step SOP.  
     - Required PPE.

     - Short embedded videos or slides.
